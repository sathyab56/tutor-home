Popular Topics

For Students
How do I get paid?
Tutors receive their payments securely through our platform. Payments are processed as follows:
  • Payment Cycle: Payouts are made on a scheduled basis (weekly/monthly) as per the agreed terms.
  • Payment Methods: You can receive payments via bank transfer, UPI, or Razorpay payout.
  • Dashboard Tracking: You can track your earnings and payment status in your tutor dashboard.
  • Deductions: Any platform fees or taxes (if applicable) will be deducted before payout.
How can I update my profile?
You can easily update your profile by following these steps:
  • Log in to your account and go to the Dashboard.
  • Click on "Profile Settings" or "Edit Profile".
  • Update your name, bio, qualifications, experience, profile picture, and other details.
  • Click "Save Changes" to apply the updates.
